Micro-Shrink: Desk World

one-sentence pitch: A minimalist two-level game where you navigate a shrunken world on a desk through text choices and hidden object discovery.

Micro-Shrink: Desk World is a short experimental game built as a text adventure followed by a hidden object challenge. In the first level, the player explores a cluttered desk as a tiny character, making branching choices and collecting tools needed to survive a dangerous desk fan. The second level shifts perspective into a visual hidden-object scene where the player must locate small everyday items before time runs out. The game focuses on clear state-based design, simple mechanics, and a clean UI built entirely through code.

  • genre: text adventure / hidden object
  • engine/tools: Unity (C#), TextMeshPro
  • features: branching narrative, inventory system, random events, click-based hidden objects

Tadpole Rescue: Wiggle Squad

One-Sentence Pitch: A Stylized 2D Underwater Platformer Where a Frog Dad Rescues Three Lost Tadpoles and Escapes via a Lily Pad Exit.

You Play as a Frog on a Rescue Mission Through a Whimsical Pixel-Art Pond. Collect All Three Tadpoles, Then Grab the Bubble Key to Unlock the Final Path. Avoid Crabs and Bubble Mines as You Platform Across the Level. If You Lose All Health or Fall, the Rescue Fails and You Restart.

  • Genre: 2D Platformer
  • Engine/Tools: Unity (2D), C#, TextMeshPro
  • Features: Tadpole Collectibles (3), Key + Locked Exit, Health Pickups, Enemies (Crabs) and Hazards (Bubble Mines), Win/Lose + Restart/Credits Screens
